ABOUT US




GlassTal Company was founded in 2018, building on a proven legacy of hands-on expertise that began in 1999. Today, we stand as a trusted name in Egypt’s construction and architectural finishing sector, delivering high-performing solutions defined by consistent quality, technical precision, and reliable delivery.


We started with glass facades, stainless steel and glass handrails, tempered glass systems, cladding works. Driven by craftsmanship, innovation, and disciplined project execution, we support landmark developments across Egypt, bringing durability, refined aesthetics, and engineering clarity to every detail.


As the mother company of an integrated multi-company ecosystem, GlassTal unites specialized entities under one umbrella to deliver end-to-end capability across project needs: Al-Borouj for construction works, Arcade for importing and fabricating stone slabs and veneer (sourced from India and Turkey), IBA for landscaping and general supplies, and DecorationARTS for interior design and decorative finishing—working together as one coordinated force to serve our clients efficiently and seamlessly
